Game introduction

"World of Warcraft" (World of Warcraft) is the first online game produced by the famous game company Blizzard Entertainment, which is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game. The game is based on the story of the real-time strategy game "Warcraft" produced by the company as its historical background. Relying on the historical events and heroes of Warcraft, World of Warcraft has a complete historical background timeline. [1] Players adventure in World of Warcraft, complete tasks, new adventures, explore unknown worlds, conquer monsters, etc.

After the release of "Warcraft III: Frozen Throne" in 2003, Blizzard Entertainment officially announced the development plan for "World of Warcraft" (which had been secretly developed for several years before). World of Warcraft was publicly tested in North America in 2004 and was released in the United States, New Zealand, Canada, Australia and Mexico on November 23 of the same year. As of the end of 2008, there were more than 11.5 million paid users of World of Warcraft worldwide and successfully entered the Guinness Book of World Records. In April 2008, World of Warcraft accounted for 62% of the MMORPG market.As of January 2014, the total number of accounts created in the world has exceeded 100 million (including demo accounts), and characters have reached 500 million. A total of 244 countries and regions are playing "World of Warcraft", including Antarctica, Bahamas, Christmas Island, and Tokelau.
